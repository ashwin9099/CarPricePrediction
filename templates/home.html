Machine Learning

Machine learning (ML) is an umbrella term for solving problems for which development of algorithms by human programmers would be cost-prohibitive, and instead the problems are solved by helping machines 'discover' their 'own' algorithms,[1] without needing to be explicitly told what to do by any human-developed algorithms.[2] When there was a vast amount of potential answers, the correct ones needed to be labeled as valid by human labelers initially and human supervision was needed.
